STATE HEALTHCARE COMPLIANCE FRAMEWORK — Version 3.2
Effective: January 1, 2025

Section 4.1 — TELEHEALTH SERVICES
- Telehealth permitted for follow-up visits only
- Video consultation required (audio-only not permitted)
- Provider must be licensed in patient's state
- Prescriptions via telehealth: Established patients only
- Documentation: Standard visit notes required

Section 4.2 — DATA SECURITY (HIPAA Aligned)
- Encryption: AES-128 minimum for data at rest
- Access controls: Role-based access required
- Audit logs: Retained for 3 years
- Breach notification: Within 60 days of discovery
- Risk assessment: Annual requirement
- Business Associate Agreements: Required for all vendors

Section 4.3 — PATIENT SAFETY REPORTING
- Adverse events: Report within 72 hours
- Near-miss reporting: Voluntary, quarterly summary
- Root cause analysis: Required for sentinel events
- Patient safety committee: Quarterly meetings

Section 4.4 — STAFFING REQUIREMENTS
- Nurse-to-patient ratio: 1:6 for medical-surgical units
- Physician supervision: On-site during operating hours
- Continuing education: 30 hours annually per clinician
- Credentialing: Every 3 years

Section 4.5 — BILLING AND COMPLIANCE
- Claim submission deadline: 90 days from service
- Prior authorization: Required for specialty referrals
- Audit frequency: Random 5% sample annually
- Penalty for fraudulent claims: Up to $50,000 per incident
