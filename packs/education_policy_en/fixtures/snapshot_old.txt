LINCOLN UNIFIED SCHOOL DISTRICT
BOARD OF EDUCATION — REGULAR MEETING MINUTES
December 10, 2025

POLICY UPDATES APPROVED:

Policy 6140 — CURRICULUM FRAMEWORK
- Core subjects: English, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies
- Electives: Art, Music, Physical Education, Foreign Language
- Technology integration: 1 computer lab per school
- Class size: Maximum 30 students per classroom
- Textbook adoption cycle: Every 6 years
- Standardized testing: State assessments in grades 3, 5, 8

Policy 5131 — STUDENT CONDUCT & SAFETY
- Zero-tolerance policy for weapons and controlled substances
- Bullying prevention: Annual awareness week
- Cell phone policy: Devices prohibited during instructional time
- Visitor policy: Sign-in at front office required
- Emergency drills: Fire drill monthly, lockdown drill quarterly

Policy 4112 — STAFF DEVELOPMENT
- Professional development: 20 hours annually required
- Teacher evaluation: Annual classroom observation
- Substitute teacher requirements: State credential required
- Staff-to-student ratio: 1:25 for general education

Policy 3100 — BUDGET ALLOCATION
- Per-pupil spending: $9,200 per student
- Technology budget: $150,000 annually
- Facilities maintenance: $500,000 reserve fund
- Transportation: 12 bus routes serving district
- Free/reduced lunch program: Income verification annually

Policy 6163 — SPECIAL EDUCATION
- IEP review cycle: Annual
- Inclusion model: Pull-out services for qualifying students
- Aide staffing: 1 aide per 8 students with IEPs
- Assistive technology: Available upon request with documentation
